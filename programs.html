A Day of Kindness and Compassion

Supporting Orphans at St. Maria de Matias Center, Inc.

The outreach program consisted of various activities such as prayers, greetings, intermission numbers, games, food, and socializing with students. Different groups participated in the activities, and the program was led by different committees and emcees.

Steps on how it happened

1. Program Opening

• It began with a prayer to ask for guidance and blessings.

• Followed by greetings and opening the program with opening remarks.

2. First Intermission Number There is an initial performance or as entertainment.

3.Games and Sports

Game:A balloon will be floated and you must wear clothes before it falls.

• Extend the Material:Find long objects to form the longest & longest line.

• Head, Shoulders, Knees, Toes, and Cup: Follow the commands and grab the cup when the word "Cup!" is said.

• Do Not Touch the Color:Walk or do an activity while avoiding the forbidden colors.

4. Food and Socializing.

• Food was handled by the food committee • There was socializing with students to strengthen the connection.

5. Second Intermission Number.

• A dance as an additional performance.

6. Photo-taking and Closing of the Program

• There was a photo-taking to commemorate the occasion.

• This was followed by closing remarks as the end of the program.

7. Jamming Session

• As the final part, there was a jamming session to enjoy and celebrate together.

The program was made fun and meaningful by combining games, and food,
