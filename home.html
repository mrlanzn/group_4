A Day of Kindness and Compassion

Supporting Orphans at St. Maria de Matias Center, Inc.

Summary of the program

The students of Marist from 9-St. Paul selected St. Maria de Matthias as the site for their outreach program, which was part of their PT in AP and RELIGION. Through careful planning over a month, we created engaging activities that provided both educational value and joyful experiences for the children, fostering meaningful connections between our students and the community.
